N0tail congratulated OG fan,
who won $826 000.

Dota 2
7 days ago

Last August OG won their second The International champions title in a row. However, OG players were not the only ones who have raised a lot of cash out of this. OG fan Daniel Foreman from Australia was not able to visit the biggest Dota 2 event due to visa issues. But sometimes what you lose on the swings, you gain on the roundabouts.

I have no idea why my visa apply was rejected two times. I did everything I need including hotel and ticket bookings," told Daniel.

All bookings were insured and Foreman fully recovered his money. After this, he decided to bet on OG win at The International 9 before the event started. He bet $10,000, which is a big sum, but how did he win that much? The thing is bookmakers offer high odds on even winner.

I have spent just a little time on looking for a bookmaker. I saw the highest odds on cyber.bet, read the feedback, went all in and never looked back
Daniel Foreman and his favorite Dota 2 captain

Cyber.bet had the highest odds on OG to win The International 9 – 82.6. OG were not favorites of the event as they backdoored their way into TI and were even close to play regional qualifiers. Even with this, 82.6 odds were at an average twice as much as other bookmakers offered.

Such news even reached OG players themselves. Of course, they won much more money, but they have spent a lifetime getting there. N0tail was the one who reacted on Daniel’s gain.

This is true as sometimes betting is much easier way to win than play by yourself. However, it is worth mentioning that players must not bet on their own matches as they can let their fans down and be involved in big uproar.
